About Luxora Design House Ltd.

Founded in 2025 by Acacia Mahon and Shawn Mahon, Luxora Design House Ltd. is more than a brand — it’s a lifestyle. Every space we touch is crafted not just with luxury in mind, but with intention, story, and emotional resonance.

What began as a vision to blend architectural mastery with soul-level healing has become a full-scale design empire. We build homes that protect, empower, and inspire — built for beauty, and born from purpose.

Core Values

  • Integrity in every detail
  • Luxury with emotional depth
  • Innovation with timeless style
  • Designing for empowerment and healing
